Animation Best Practices

‚ö°
Performance First
Use transform and opacity for animations (GPU-accelerated). Avoid animating width, height, or position properties.
üéØ
Purpose-Driven
Every animation should serve a purpose: draw attention, provide feedback, or establish hierarchy. Never animate just for show.
‚è±Ô∏è
Timing Matters
Fast micro-interactions (200ms), moderate transitions (300-400ms), entrance animations (500-600ms). Never exceed 1 second.
üîÑ
Respect Motion Preferences
Always respect prefers-reduced-motion. Provide static alternatives for users with motion sensitivity.
üé®
Easing is Everything
Use ease-out for entrances, ease-in for exits, and ease-in-out for loops. Custom cubic-bezier curves add personality.
üìê
Consistent System
Define a duration scale (200ms, 300ms, 600ms) and reuse across the app. Consistency builds familiarity and feels polished.
